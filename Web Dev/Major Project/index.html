About the Project

  • What is the project about? The Real-Time Stock Market Dashboard is a web application that provides live updates and analytics of stock market data, specifically focused on Indian market indices like Nifty and Sensex. It offers users real-time data, visualizations (charts/graphs), and insights to make informed investment decisions. The platform also includes customizable alerts, market trends, and a responsive, user-friendly interface. The system uses Node.js, React.js, and real-time data streaming via Kafka.

  • Why the need? In the fast-paced world of stock trading, timely and accurate information is crucial. The Real-Time Stock Market Dashboard addresses the need for a centralized platform that provides live market updates, trends, and data analytics. It helps investors make informed decisions, track market performance, and stay ahead of market fluctuations, especially in the volatile Indian stock market, ensuring they never miss out on important insights.

  • How are we offering solutions? By using modern technologies like Kafka, MongoDB, and React.js to offer real-time market updates, interactive data visualizations, custom alerts, and a user-friendly interface. The platform is scalable with cloud hosting on AWS, ensuring reliability and efficient tracking of stock trends.
